Crystal Collector Game

By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the Random Generator. You lose the game if your total score goes above the Random Generator. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
